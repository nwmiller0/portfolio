Writing Examples

Here are some examples of writing that I've done in my course work.

Database Proposal

This selection of writing comes from my ITEC 3650 course. It was a database proposal that I wrote for our clients when working to build a pizza site for a fictional company. Even though we weren't working for "real" clients this gave me experience writing for the workplace.

Intruction Manual

This piece of writing come from my Technical Writing class. The objective of the assignment was to write technical intructions in detail. I worked with a group to write intructions for how to properly inspect grenades. I really enjoyed this process. I thought our team worked well together and we were able to stay on track with our work schedule. One thing I liked about this was discovering how to write about something you have no idea about. I had to do a lot of research to properly understand the subject and the history of it.
